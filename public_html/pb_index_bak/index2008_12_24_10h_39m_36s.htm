About Us

ABC Cross-Country Ski Club was founded in 1972 by a group of cross-country skiers looking to expand their horizons and meet new people who shared a love for the sport. The mission of ABC Cross-Country Ski Club is to provide members with the opportunity to ski a variety of trails in the Sportstown area and to promote cross-country skiing as an excellent source of physical activity. We accept members of all ages and ability levels and we hope you will join us for winter fun and fitness!
